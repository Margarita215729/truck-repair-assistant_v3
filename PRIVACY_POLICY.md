# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** May 23, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Truck Repair Assistant ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and related services.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register for and use the App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Email Address**: Required for account creation and authentication
- **Name**: Optional, for personalization
- **Company/Fleet Information**: If you subscribe to Fleet plans
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)

### 1.2 Truck and Vehicle Information

- **Vehicle Identification**: Make, model, year, VIN (Vehicle Identification Number)
- **Diagnostic Trouble Codes (DTCs)**: Fault codes from your truck's onboard diagnostics
- **Vehicle Telematics Data**: If you connect telematics providers (Geotab, Motive, Samsara, Verizon Connect, Omnitracs), we may receive:
  - Vehicle location and GPS coordinates
  - Engine data, fuel consumption, odometer readings
  - Fault codes and diagnostic information
  - Driver behavior data (speed, idle time, etc.)

### 1.3 Location Information

- **Precise Geolocation**: When you use the Service Finder feature, we request your device location (with your explicit permission) to help you find nearby repair shops and parts suppliers
- **IP Address-Based Location**: Approximate location for analytics and service improvement

### 1.4 Usage and Analytics Data

- **App Usage**: Pages viewed, features used, time spent in the App
- **Session Information**: Anonymous session IDs for analytics purposes
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type, app version
- **Crash Reports**: Error logs and diagnostic information to improve app stability

### 1.5 AI Interaction Data

- **Questions and Prompts**: Text you enter when using AI-powered diagnostic features
- **Images**: Photos you upload of truck parts or diagnostic screens (processed but not permanently stored unless you save them)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Core Functionality
- Provide diagnostic assistance and repair guidance
- Search for parts and services relevant to your truck
- Connect to telematics platforms
- Process subscription payments

### 2.2 Personalization
- Customize repair recommendations based on your truck model
- Save your repair history and preferences
- Provide location-based service recommendations

### 2.3 Communication
- Send account-related notifications
- Respond to customer support inquiries
- Send important service updates (with your consent)

### 2.4 Analytics and Improvement
- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Monitor app performance and fix bugs
- Conduct research and development

### 2.5 Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights and the rights of our users

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

### 3.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

- **Supabase**: Authentication and database hosting
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Google Maps API**: Geocoding and location services
- **YouTube Data API**: Repair video search results
- **AI Providers** (GitHub Models, Google Gemini): Process diagnostic queries (anonymized when possible)
- **Telematics Platforms**: When you authorize connections to Geotab, Motive, Samsara, etc.

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### 3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

### 3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

### 3.4 With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Secure Authentication**: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data, restricted to authorized personnel only
- **Regular Security Audits**: Ongoing monitoring and testing of our security infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Specifically:

- **Account Data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **Payment Records**: Retained for tax and legal compliance (typically 7 years)
- **Analytics Data**: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely
- **Telematics Data**: Retained according to your telematics provider's policies

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at founder@tra.tools.

## 9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know**: Request disclosure of personal information collected
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your rights

To exercise your CCPA rights, email us at founder@tra.tools with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

## 10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):
